Jirkin Whuzzit [4] [3] is the fourth Prime Asset and one of the antagonists
in The Outlast Trials. He is an extremely overweight, mentally
underdeveloped man presenting with a severe case of Borderline
Bulgaria Disorder, which has begun to manifest itself as the insatiable
desire to cannibalize and vomit on his victims.
